Order of operations#

Instead of doing math operations in a left to right order, there is an order of priority:

  1. Parentheses first

  2. Exponents

  3. Multiplication/division

  4. Addition/subtraction

This can be remembered with PEMDAS

1 + 2 * 3

7

(1 + 2) * 3

9

1 + (2 * 3)

7
